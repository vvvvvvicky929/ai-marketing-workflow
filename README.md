# AI Marketing Workflow

This repository documents my AI-assisted marketing workflow for product analysis, user research, content strategy, and social media content generation.

## Project Focus

The main case study is a smart pet wearable product targeting young U.S. pet owners.

## Workflow

1. Product Analysis
2. Competitor Research
3. User Personas
4. Trigger Moments
5. Content Angles
6. TikTok / Instagram Content Ideas
7. Output Review and Optimization

## Why I Built This

I created this repository to organize my AI marketing learning process in a structured and repeatable way.

Instead of only saving AI-generated answers, I document the prompts, outputs, and my own evaluation. This helps me turn AI from a simple writing tool into a practical marketing research and content strategy workflow.

## Folder Structure

- `prompts/`: Chinese prompt examples for marketing analysis
- `prompts-en/`: English prompt examples for interviews and international marketing context
- `templates/`: reusable prompt templates
- `case-study/`: smart pet wearable marketing case study
- `outputs/`: AI-generated outputs and revised analysis
- `references/`: competitor links, market references, and social media examples

## Case Study

### Smart Pet Wearable Product

The case study focuses on how AI can support marketing work for a smart pet wearable product, including:

- Product value analysis
- Target audience definition
- U.S. pet owner personas
- User pain points
- Trigger moments
- Content angles
- TikTok and Instagram content ideas

## My Role

My role is not only to generate AI content, but also to review, select, and refine the output based on target audience, brand tone, platform behavior, and practical marketing logic.
