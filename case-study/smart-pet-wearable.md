# Smart Pet Wearable AI Marketing Workflow

## Product

A smart pet wearable product that helps pet owners track location, activity, and health-related signals.

## Target Audience

Young U.S. pet owners, especially urban professionals, outdoor pet owners, and health-conscious pet parents.

## Core Value

The product provides reassurance, safety, and daily health awareness for pet owners.

## AI Workflow

### 1. Product Analysis

Use AI to summarize the product value, key features, and possible market positioning.

### 2. User Personas

Use AI to generate target user personas based on lifestyle, pet care behavior, pain points, and purchase motivations.

### 3. Trigger Moments

Identify moments that may push users to consider buying the product, such as losing sight of a pet, noticing unusual behavior, or preparing for outdoor activities.

### 4. Content Angles

Develop marketing content from safety, health, emotional connection, lifestyle, and education perspectives.

### 5. Social Media Output

Translate insights into TikTok and Instagram content ideas, including hooks, short video scripts, and captions.

## My Review

AI output is not used directly as the final answer. I review and refine the results based on:

- Whether the insight matches the target audience
- Whether the message fits the brand tone
- Whether the content works for TikTok and Instagram
- Whether the output is specific enough to guide real marketing content
